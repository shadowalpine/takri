About Takri

Takri is an ancient Brahmic script that was historically used in the Western Himalayas, particularly in the regions of Himachal Pradesh, Jammu and Kashmir, and parts of Punjab. This script has been an integral part of the cultural and literary heritage of the hill regions, used for writing various languages including Chambeali, Dogri, and others.

Despite its historical significance, Takri script has been gradually declining in usage. Our mission is to preserve this valuable piece of cultural heritage by bringing it into the digital age, making it accessible to learners and researchers worldwide.
